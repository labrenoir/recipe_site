Chocolate Cake Recipe

"A simple recipe for a tasty cake, if you like chocolate."

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients:

black chocolate: 200 g

sugar: 150 g

butter: 200 g

eggs: 4

flour: 80 g

Instructions:

1

melt chocolaate and butter together

2

mix sugar, eggs and flour

3

pre-heat oven 350 degrees

4

combine all ingredients in large bowl

5

mix until smooth

6

bake in oven
